Students often struggle to manage multiple subjects, upcoming deadlines, incomplete tasks, and their daily study schedule. Most study-planning tools are either too complex, require online access, or contain unnecessary features that overwhelm beginners.  
There is a need for a simple, lightweight, offline solution that allows students to plan their studies and track their progress without distractions. StudyBuddy CLI aims to solve this problem by providing an organized and minimal command-line study planner.

---

## ðŸ“Œ Scope of the Project
The scope of StudyBuddy CLI includes:
- Providing a command-line interface for managing study-related data.
- Allowing users to add, view, and organize subjects.
- Tracking tasks with due dates and priority levels.
- Logging daily study sessions for each subject.
- Storing all data using JSON files for easy retrieval.
- Generating weekly study reports.
- Offering simple study suggestions based on performance.

The project does **not** include advanced GUI features, cloud syncing, or mobile integration. It focuses strictly on essential planning and tracking.
